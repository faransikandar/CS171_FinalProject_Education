Why are students dropping out from schools?

  • Financial Constraints:

    Sending the children to school might be too expensive.

  • Distance to Schools:

    Traveling to school might take up too much time. Sometimes it could be dangerous for children, especially girls to walk to a faraway school alone.

  • Household's preferences:

    Parents might have preference for boys to attend schools over girls. Or they could prefer to send kids that they percieved to be smarter and more talented to attend schools.
